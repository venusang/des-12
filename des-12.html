Score Criteria
4
 The question or task is completed correctly and contains additional, unexpected, or outstanding features.
3
 The question or task is compelted correctly; there are no mistakes.
2
 The answer or task is partly correct; it has no big mistakes.
1
 The student does not do the question or task, or gives an answer that has nothing to do with what is asked.
